The Year Three Thousand (TYTT) is a game I made in fifth/sixth grade. It is a futuristic multiplayer FPS shooter game.

I made it with Unity and it was my first multiplayer game I ever made. It even has user accounts and stats. Kinda cringey game but I learned a lot making it. I haven't tested the servers but they could still be working.
