Cursed to Consume is a 2D platformer I made as a school project. I started in September 2024 and currently it has one playable level.

A witch has cursed you to consume little kids. In this demo you are trying to escape from the witch's dungeon which is located below a normal house all while needing to consume some children.

It was made with Godot 4 and GD Script. For sprites/animations I used a site called pixilart.com.
